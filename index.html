Short Bio

I'm current a second-year PhD student at MIT, CSAIL, Data System Group. I'm super fortunately co-advised by Prof. Samuel Madden and Prof. Tim Kraska. Before MIT, I achieved a master degree from University of Oxford and bachelor degrees from Carnegie Mellon University. My primary research focus is to use traditional machine learning or deep learning techniques to build better query optimizers. I'm also working on hybrid transactional and analytical processing system (HTAP) and video database management systems.
